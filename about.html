About Me

I'm Daryl Smith, and my life is music.

Songwriter, Cover Artist, and a Chill Vibe Enthusiast. This is my journey.

My life is completely built around music, deeply rooted in the **Rapper-Singer style**, which fuses Rap, Hip Hop, RnB, and Pop elements. I all started back when I was just listeningâ€”immersed in the sounds of legendary rappers and soulful singers. I wasn't just casually listening; I was absorbing the flow, the lyrical gymnastics, and the deep emotions embedded in RnB melodies. That's where I discovered my deep passion for music. Before I knew it, I found myself instinctively writing **original songs and poetry**, mimicking the styles I admired. The real challenge, though, was turning those written words into an actual performance. When I first tried to translate my poems into a song, my voice wasn't developed yet, and I really struggled to sing in tune. But a good friend gave me the push I needed, telling me: **"Don't worry about sounding bad at first. Just try to reach those difficult tones, and even if the result is rough, keep pushing yourself until you naturally find the right arrangement and technique."** That simple advice was the turning point. I trained myself tirelessly, practicing those hard notes until I finally learned to sing in a way that sounded smooth and beautiful to listen to. Today, my sound is a genuine blend of the lyrical sharpness and rhythmic intensity I learned from Rap and Hip Hop, combined with the smooth vocal melodies and emotional depth of RnB and Pop. My style is focused on capturing a **raw, authentic feeling** in every track I create, ensuring the vibe is always authentic and chill.
